Build a back testing system that simulate the trade of covered call (short call) and estimate the strategy performance (e.g. annual return, maximum drawdown)

Please use thetadata v3 for fetching the required option data https://docs.thetadata.us/

Strategy
- start on the first day of the year
- execute every order at 10:00 AM EST
- sell a call with an expiration day of 45 and delta 0.2 at bid price
- roll the call if any of these conditions is met at ask price
1. the call is 21 days to expiration
2. 50% profit earned
- for each roll, consider a slippage of 0.01

Each time you sell or buy a call, make sure you append a row of data to a file (e.g. excel file) with these columns
1. Date
2. Position (Buy or sell)
3. Right (Call or Put)
4. Price of the trade
5. Day to expiration
6. Change of the account

Plot a line graph with the trend of SPY and indication of option trade on it.
1. Use green cross for sell and blue cross for buy
